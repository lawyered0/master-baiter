# Code of Conduct

## Our Purpose

This project exists to protect people from scammers and predators. Every contributor shares a responsibility to ensure this tool is used ethically and legally.

## Core Principles

1. **Protect the innocent.** This tool wastes scammers' time so they can't victimize real people. It detects predatory behavior so it can be reported to authorities. Never use it to harass, stalk, or target innocent people.

2. **Respect the law.** All predator detection is passive — we detect and report, we do not entrap. Generated reports are drafts requiring human review. Follow your jurisdiction's laws regarding scam baiting and evidence collection.

3. **Never simulate minors.** The skill must never role-play as a minor or child under any circumstances. Predator detection is classification-only.

4. **Report responsibly.** File reports through proper channels (IC3, FTC, NCMEC CyberTipline, local law enforcement). Do not publish scammer personal information publicly — let law enforcement act.

5. **Be kind to each other.** Treat fellow contributors with respect. We're all here to make the internet safer.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Using this tool to target, harass, or dox individuals
- Modifying the skill to role-play as a minor
- Filing false reports with law enforcement agencies
- Publishing scammer personal information outside of proper reporting channels
- Using collected intel for personal gain or vigilante action
- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks against contributors

## Reporting Issues

If you see this tool being misused or have concerns about contributor behavior, open a private issue or contact the maintainers directly.

## Enforcement

Violations of this code of conduct may result in removal of contributions, banning from the project, and in severe cases, reporting to relevant authorities.
